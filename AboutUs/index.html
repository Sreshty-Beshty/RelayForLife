Fighting Cancer, One Step at a Time.

Relay For Life is the world's largest fundraising movement for cancer research and support — a community event where teams walk through the night, symbolising that cancer never sleeps, and neither do we.

At Oakridge International School, Bangalore, our chapter brings together students, families, survivors, and fighters to raise awareness, funds, and hope — because no one should face cancer alone.

More Than an Event.

Every Relay For Life event is a celebration of survivors, a tribute to those we've lost, and a call to action for a future free from cancer. We walk so that no one has to face this journey alone.

At Oakridge Bangalore, our chapter is entirely student-led — organised by the Student Council and driven by passionate volunteers committed to making a tangible difference through fundraising, education, and relentless community spirit.

Partnered with the
Indian Cancer Society

Our Relay For Life chapter at Oakridge proudly operates in partnership with the Indian Cancer Society (ICS) — India's oldest and most trusted cancer NGO, founded in 1947. Together, we channel every rupee raised toward early detection, patient support, and nationwide cancer awareness.

The ICS's decades of expertise amplify our local efforts into a national impact, ensuring the funds we raise create real, measurable change for patients and families across India.

Every Walk
Funds a Future.

Our annual overnight relay at Oakridge is more than a walkathon — it's a full community experience. From high-energy Zumba sessions and games that keep the spirit alive through the night, to heartfelt luminaria ceremonies that honour survivors and those we've lost, every moment is designed to unite and inspire.

All net proceeds go directly to the Indian Cancer Society, supporting free cancer screenings, patient transport, and treatment assistance for those who need it most.

About Relay For Life

Relay For Life is a global movement dedicated to celebrating cancer survivors, remembering those lost, and fighting back against the disease. Teams gather to walk through the night in solidarity with millions of people affected by cancer.

Participants take turns walking around the track, symbolizing that cancer never sleeps — and neither does the fight against it. The event includes survivor laps, luminaria ceremonies, team activities, and fundraising initiatives supporting cancer research and patient care.

Relay For Life is more than an event. It is a community coming together to bring hope, awareness, and strength to the fight against cancer.

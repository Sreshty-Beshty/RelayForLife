Our Mission

More Than
a Walk.

Relay For Life is the world's largest fundraising movement for cancer research — a night-long community event where teams walk in solidarity, because cancer never sleeps, and neither do we.

At Oakridge Bangalore, our chapter is entirely student-led, driven by the Student Council and hundreds of passionate volunteers.
